Understanding Sound Levels

Safe (30-60 dB)

Normal conversation, quiet office, library. Safe for prolonged exposure.

Moderate (60-80 dB)

Traffic, restaurant, vacuum cleaner. Generally safe with occasional breaks.

Caution (80-90 dB)

Heavy traffic, power tools. Limit exposure time and consider hearing protection.

Danger (90-110 dB)

Concerts, machinery, power equipment. Hearing protection required.

Critical (110+ dB)

Rock concerts, sirens, firearms. Immediate hearing damage risk.
